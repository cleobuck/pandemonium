   "Hi, my name's Aaron. A few years ago, I used to welcome couchsurfers at my appartment in Darwin. One day, a girl showed up with a handpan. After a few sessions hitting on this wonderful creature, I fell in love and decided to buy the handpan.

    I ended up quitting my job, and went from working in the navy to full gypsie. I now tour the world with my handpan, didjeridoos and guitar in search of amazing sounds and adventures. I live in my van, busk and throw concerts for a living."
